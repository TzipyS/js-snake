About the game Snake

Snake is one of the most classic and well-known arcade games in the world. The goal of the game is simple but challenging - to control the snake, eat food that appears on the screen, and grow as you progress through the game. Each time the snake eats an item, it grows longer and the game becomes more challenging. The player must avoid colliding with walls or the snake itself, and try to achieve the highest score. The game combines quick thinking, concentration and route planning, and is suitable for all ages. Despite its simplicity, Snake requires quick reactions and strategy to continue progressing and breaking records. In the version on this website, you can enjoy a fun, simple and accessible gaming experience - and try to improve your score every time you play.
